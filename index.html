About Me

I am a passionate and highly motivated Data Science student, currently pursuing M.Sc. in Data Science from Chandigarh University, with a strong foundation built through a BCA from Graphic Era Hill University.

With hands-on experience in machine learning, data analysis, and web development, I enjoy solving real-world problems by combining analytical thinking and creative coding. I have built several projects including a heart disease prediction model, task manager web app, chatbot application, and more — showcasing my skills in Python, Flask, Node.js, MongoDB, and modern frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, and Tailwind.

My ability to manage both backend and frontend, paired with my understanding of data science tools like Pandas, NumPy, and Scikit-learn, makes me capable of building full-stack and intelligent solutions. I'm constantly exploring new technologies, eager to learn, and passionate about making data-driven decisions that create meaningful impact.

Objective

My goal is to become a skilled and reliable Data Scientist who contributes to solving complex business challenges using the power of data.

I aim to apply my knowledge of Python, machine learning, SQL, and data visualization to extract actionable insights and support data-driven decisions. I’m especially interested in building intelligent systems that automate tasks, improve efficiency, and deliver value to users.

I am looking for opportunities to grow in a professional environment where I can continue learning, collaborating with talented teams, and applying my skills in real-world scenarios.
